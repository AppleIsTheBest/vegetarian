Sustainability

LED Lights

Our restaurant uses LED lights throughout our facility to save energy costs and emphasise the use of efficient electricity use. Our cost savings are then reinvested to improve customer satisfaction.

Vegetarian Cuisine

Our world-class selection of vegetarian dishes eliminates the use of meat in our dishes. By eliminating meat, we can cater to our customers while helping factor our the costs of raising farm animals, providing them with water, transportation, and other carbon-emmitting activities.


Learn More

Preparation Processes

Our world-class preparation processes align with USDA standards to ensure safety, health, and sustainability. By ensuring our processes eliminate carbon emmissions at every step, our customers can enjoy food free of environmental detriments.


Learn More

Farm-to-Table

Our farm-to-table approach is one of care, ensuring food free of environmental toxins, including pesticides and preservatives. Our food is sourced only from the highest-quality American farms. This ensures a minimal impact on the environment while ensuring high-quality food.
